Getting Started with My99Exch: Register, Login, and First Bet

So you want a My99Exch account. Look, the whole process takes about 10 minutes and most of that is waiting for the UPI payment to confirm. Here's how it actually works.

Registration

Honestly? It's just a WhatsApp conversation. You message the number, say you want a new ID, pick a deposit amount, pay via UPI or Paytm, and you get your username and password. We get this question a lot, and yeah, that's really all there is to it. The My99Exch register page has the current WhatsApp number and minimum deposit details.

First Login

Once you've got credentials, head to the login page. Bookmark it because the URL can change occasionally. The My99Exch login page always has the latest working URL plus what to do if you can't get in. First thing after login: change your password. Just do it. Takes 30 seconds.

The App

Basically everyone uses the app instead of the browser. It's just faster and the bet placement is smoother. From what we've seen, about 90% of active users are on mobile. The My99Exch app page has download links. It's a small APK, installs in under a minute on any Android phone. No Play Store, just direct download.

Quick tip from us: enable notifications in the app settings. You'll get alerts when markets open for upcoming matches, which is handy during IPL when there's a match almost every day.

Not gonna lie, the app design isn't going to win any beauty contests. But it works, it's fast, and that's what counts when you're trying to place a bet during a live match.
